Temperature

Temperature influences crop growth and development. Different crops have optimal temperature ranges, and deviations can impact plant metabolism, flowering, and yield.

Min Temperature

Minimum temperature affects frost risk.

Max Temperature

Maximum temperature influences heat stress. Both extremes can harm crops, affecting yield and quality.

Wind Speed

Wind can impact crop pollination, water evaporation rates, and plant structure.

Wind Degree

Wind direction is crucial for pesticide application and disease management in agriculture.

Sunset

Day length affects flowering and fruiting in many crops. Knowledge of sunset and sunrise times aids in planning agricultural activities, optimizing daylight exposure for plants.

Sunrise

Day length affects flowering and fruiting in many crops. Knowledge of sunset and sunrise times aids in planning agricultural activities, optimizing daylight exposure for plants.

Cloud PCT

Cloud cover influences sunlight availability for photosynthesis. Excessive cloudiness can reduce crop productivity by limiting the energy received by plants.

Feels Like

The "feels like" temperature accounts for factors like humidity and wind, providing a more accurate representation of how weather conditions affect human and plant comfort.

Humidity

Humidity influences water uptake, transpiration, and disease susceptibility in plants. Balancing humidity is crucial for optimizing plant health and mitigating fungal diseases.
